# Solid IP

## Character Definition

Solid is the recurring visual IP for article illustrations in this fork.

Solid should appear by default in every image. Solid is not a mascot, sticker, or cute decoration. Solid is the deadpan worker seriously participating in the system on the page.

## Appearance

- Small solid-black creature.
- White dot eyes.
- Thin legs, sometimes thin arms.
- **Body is a simple hexagon**: a small six-sided black block or tile, hand-drawn with a slightly wobbly outline.
- The hexagon can tilt, lean, or compress a little for action, but the silhouette should stay recognizably hexagonal.
- Blank, calm, serious expression on the hexagon face.
- Same visual language as the original Solid family: dot eyes, thin limbs, deadpan operator energy — only the body silhouette changes.

## Shape Variants

Default body shape is **hexagon**. Other characters in the same family may use a different simple silhouette — circle, triangle, pentagon, square — while keeping the same dot eyes, thin legs, and serious operator role.

Unless the user asks for another shape, always draw the default hexagon body.

## Personality

- Very serious while doing something slightly absurd.
- Like a quiet system operator.
- Dry humor, never cute for cuteness' sake.
- A little awkward, but not stupid.
- Feels responsible for a real job inside a whiteboard sketch.

## Common Responsibilities

Make Solid perform the core action:

- carrying raw material
- pulling wires that merge information sources
- getting stuck at a breakpoint
- operating a "judgment" lever inside a machine
- becoming a filtering funnel
- cutting or reshaping source material
- stamping handoff copy
- dragging a handoff path
- holding a warning sign near a pitfall
- reaching from a hole but missing the content
- building a bridge, opening a gate, sorting, recording, repairing, weighing, or feeding an odd device

## Prohibited

- Do not make Solid an overly cute mascot.
- Do not make Solid a children's cartoon character.
- Do not add complex clothing, meme expressions, shiny eyes, or decorative accessories.
- Do not let Solid stand in a corner watching.
- Do not let Solid overpower the conceptual structure.
- Do not make Solid too commercial, too rounded, or too polished.
- Do not default to a round blob, bean, cylinder, or soft organic body. The default silhouette is a hexagon.
- Do not draw a perfectly sharp vector hexagon. Keep the hand-drawn, slightly uneven pen-line feel.

## Judgment Standard

If the core metaphor still works perfectly after removing Solid, Solid is too decorative. Rewrite the prompt so Solid becomes the subject performing the conceptual action.

If the hexagon body is missing or unreadable, regenerate with a clearer six-sided silhouette.
